About us

What is ORAS?

Who are we? Why is our name like how it is? It is simpler than you think.We are a group of friends who one day decided to take a risk and open a restaurant.We had trouble choosing the name, so we just put the first thing that came into our minds as the name.That's it!

Our Mission

We at ORAS are dedicated to becoming your number one choice, known for the freshest, tastiest and heathiest dough. Pinza aims to send good vibes and deeds back into our community, remaining a champion for quality, for value, and for universal kindness in our thoughts and actions.
